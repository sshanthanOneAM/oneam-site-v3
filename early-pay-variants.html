New to early pay?

If this is the first time you've heard the term, you're not alone. Most business owners haven't — and that's exactly why we built OneAM.

Early pay is a simple idea: instead of waiting 30, 60, or 90 days for your customer to pay an invoice, you get the cash now from an institutional investor. Your customer still pays on their normal schedule. You skip the wait.
